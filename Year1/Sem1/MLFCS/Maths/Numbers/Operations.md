Examples of operations include:
- Addition
- Multiplication
- Exponent

Addtion is:
- **commutative**
	a+b = b+a
- **associative**
	a+(b+c) = (a+b)+c
- **has a natural element**
	a+0 =a

Multiplication is
- **commutative**
	ab = ba
-  **associative**
	a(bc) = (ab)c
- **has a natural element**
	a*1 =a

Multiplication distributes over addition
- a(b+c) = a*b + a*c
and has a annihalating element
- $a*0 = 0$
